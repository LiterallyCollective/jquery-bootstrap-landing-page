Vitamin Angels

Give one year of lifesaving vitamin A to a child in need

150 million children don’t get the Vitamin A they need. Our bodies rely on vitamin A for many purposes, all of which are essential for day-to-day activities and long-term health. When a child lacks this nutrient during the early stages of development, they can get sick, experience stunted growth, go blind and even die. Families with children in developing countries who are struggling with food insecurity are most at risk, because purchasing or growing vitamin A-rich foods isn’t always possible.

Your donation today gives vitamin A to a child in need for one year through the nonprofit Vitamin Angels. Operating in 70 countries around the world, Vitamin Angels seeks out the most vulnerable, and too often forgotten: pregnant women, infants, and children who live beyond the margins.

Feed the Children

Provide a nutritious meal to a child in need

Hunger is a global problem and Feed the Children operates in countries around the world where they can make the biggest impact. Internationally, Feed the Children implements a Child-Focused Community Development (CFCD) approach, which includes program activities in the areas of food and nutrition, health and water, education, and livelihoods. The organization works with vulnerable and at-risk children, their caregivers, and communities to cultivate the social, economic, cultural, and environmental conditions needed for families to thrive.

Your donation today provides a child in need with a nutritious meal. Through Feed The Children, students experiencing homelessness are enrolled in a nutrition program and provided a sustainable source of nutritious foods so they can focus on.

Baby2Baby

Provide a day’s worth of diapers to a baby in poverty

Baby2Baby is a non-profit organization that provides children living in poverty, ages 0-12 years, with diapers, clothing and all the basic necessities that every child deserves. Children living in poverty are the most vulnerable in any crisis, and COVID-19 is no exception. 1 in 3 families in this country were already choosing between diapers and food before the pandemic, and now the families that Baby2Baby serves are struggling even more due to months of lost income, millions of lost jobs and increased lack of access to these critical items.

When Baby2Baby can provide these critical items, families can use their extremely limited funds to focus on food and shelter. The need is growing every day, and your support will help them to continue providing children with the items they need to survive during this unprecedented time.

Doctors Without Borders

Enable medical care for an individual battling COVID-19

In the face of the unprecedented global crisis of the coronavirus pandemic, Doctors Without Borders has created the COVID-19 Crisis Fund. The fund aims to raise urgently-needed money for their global emergency response to the new coronavirus pandemic and its consequences.

The objectives of their global response to COVID-19 are to: slow down and stop or delay transmission of the virus and provide care for patients (particularly the seriously ill); minimize the impact on health systems and communities; disseminate information on forms of infection prevention and risks; enhance water, hygiene and sanitation supplies and activities; and provide palliative care for the most seriously ill patients in countries with limited resources.

Your donation will enable urgently-needed medical care and support to be set up or continued in more than 70 countries.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Snap Day In?

Formerly known as Snap CAMP Day Out, Snap Day In is our annual global day of service on World Kindness Day! On this day, Snap team members across the world have the option to dedicate a portion of the workday to participating in at-home volunteer projects planned by Snap Philanthropy.

The goals of Snap Day In are as follows:

  • Empower our Snap family across all office locations to make a difference, while winning from home
  • Do our part to spread kindness and positivity in our local communities
  • Organize a variety of service opportunities supporting nonprofits focused on youth, education, and arts
  • Rally the team around a meaningful and fun day of service that creates a shared experience, despite not being able to be together in person

In what others ways does Snap give back?

Snap spreads positivity and kindness in the world by giving back. We care deeply about being a good neighbor and thoughtfully consider how we can make the greatest impact by bringing together our Snap family with our local communities. Snap does more than write checks. We strengthen and promote the well-being of the neighborhoods where we work through volunteering and engaging in partnerships with nonprofits focusing on our three philanthropic pillars — youth, education and the arts. To learn more about the ways in which Snap makes an impact, please visit https://citizen.snap.com/giving.

Who is Gifts for Good?

Gifts for Good is the first corporate gifting company focused solely on social impact. Each gift on their site supports over 50 different nonprofits and social enterprises affecting change in 65 countries around the globe.
